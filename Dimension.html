Dimensions Explained

1D - Point
The foundation of geometry - a single location in space with no length, width, or height.
1D - Line
One-dimensional space with only length. Movement exists only in one direction.
2D - Plane
Two dimensions: length and width. This is the plane of paper, drawings, and flat surfaces.
3D - Space
Three dimensions: length, width, and height. The physical space we live in and experience daily.
4D - Space-Time
The fourth dimension is time, combining 3D space with temporal flow in the fabric of reality.
5D+ - Beyond
Theoretical dimensions beyond our perception, explored in string theory and quantum physics.
